About Me

My name is Rebeccah Blau and I am 21 years old. I was born in Madison, Wisconsin but grew up in Maryland.

I am currently a senior at the University of Miami and I am studying Journalism and Theatre. I transferred to UMiami at the beginning of my sophomore year and I love it here. I am both excited and nervous to be graduating in the spring.

In my free time I enjoy reading, spending time with friends, and listening to music.
